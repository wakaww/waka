About

Waka Worldwide is a non-profit organization that is committed to express God’s love and care by providing homes and basic needs for homeless children and teenaged mothers. With an international perspective, we partner with local organizations across the globe to provide shelter, food, medical care, and other basic needs for homeless youth.

Vision:
To restore, reconcile, and transform communities by rescuing youth off the streets and equipping them to live fully into God’s purposes.
